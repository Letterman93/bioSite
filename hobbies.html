What inspires Samantha

Samantha's hobbies reflect how she recharges, keeps learning, and builds meaningful time with family.

Hobby Image 1

Playing games

Samantha enjoys PC gaming across competitive titles, open-world adventures, and co-op games that she can play with family and friends.

Why it matters: Gaming gives her a way to connect, unwind, and stay challenged.

Hobby Image 2

Reading

Reading gives Samantha quiet time to reflect, keep learning, and explore ideas tied to psychology and personal growth.

Why it matters: It supports her graduate studies and long-term goal of opening her own practice.

Hobby Image 3

Baking

Baking is one of Samantha's creative outlets, and she enjoys making recipes for family evenings and special celebrations.

Why it matters: It reflects her care for others and the home-centered life she is building.

Hobby Image 4

Traveling

Traveling in the camper helps Samantha and her family explore new places, experiences, and routines together.

Why it matters: It aligns with her goal to see more of the country and world before building a homestead.
