# Module 5.2 Assignment: bioSite Planning

## Project Direction

Subject: Samantha Nizam

Site angle: A story-driven bioSite focused on Samantha’s life as a wife, mother, student, traveler, and someone building a new chapter through full-time RV life and education.

Core message:
- Samantha is balancing family, education, and a mobile lifestyle while still building toward long-term goals.
- The site should feel clean, warm, personal, calm, and modern.
- The visual tone should be soft and welcoming, not flashy.

Pages planned:
- Landing page
- About page
- Hobbies page
- Style guide / pattern library

---

## Style Guide

### Color palette
- Deep plum: `#4F3674`
- Soft lavender: `#B8A0D9`
- Warm gold: `#D6A800`
- Light background gray: `#F3F3F3`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Dark text: `#2F2F2F`

### Typography
- Headings: `Georgia`, serif
- Body text: `Arial`, sans-serif
- Accent / small text: `Arial` or a similar clean sans-serif

### Layout and composition
- Generous white space
- Balanced sections with clear hierarchy
- Rounded image corners for a friendly feel
- Simple top navigation across all pages
- Soft cards or section backgrounds for emphasis

### Buttons and links
- Primary link color: `#4F3674`
- Hover color: `#D6A800`
- Underline on hover for links
- Buttons: solid `#4F3674` background with white text, or white background with deep plum border for secondary buttons

### Visual style notes
- Personal photography over stock images
- Simple, calm backgrounds
- Focus on family, growth, travel, and education
- Use subtle cards and section dividers rather than heavy borders

## Bio Draft (250+ words)

Samantha Nizam is a wife, mother, student, and full-time traveler who has embraced a lifestyle centered on family, growth, and new experiences. Over the last several years, her life has taken a path that is different from what many people would consider traditional, but that is exactly what makes her story meaningful. Instead of staying tied to one place, she and her family made the decision to simplify their lives, leave behind the normal routine of home ownership, and begin traveling full-time in a fifth wheel.

At the center of Samantha’s life is her family. She plays a major role in helping create a stable and meaningful life for their two children while also managing the challenges that come with constant movement and change. At the same time, she has continued working toward her own education, showing a strong commitment to personal growth and long-term goals. Balancing school, motherhood, travel, and day-to-day life takes patience and discipline, but those responsibilities have also helped shape who she is.

Samantha’s story is not just about travel. It is about building a life that reflects freedom, intention, and family values. She is someone who continues to adapt, learn, and move forward while staying grounded in what matters most to her. Whether she is focused on school, planning the next stop, or spending time with her family, she represents resilience and the ability to grow through change.

This site will showcase Samantha’s journey through personal accomplishments, family time, and the hobbies that keep her inspired. The landing page will introduce the story and highlight her goals. The About page will present her biography with a portrait and quick facts. The Hobbies page will share the activities that matter most to her and explain why they are meaningful. The design will be calm, warm, and welcoming, with a focus on storytelling and clear composition.
